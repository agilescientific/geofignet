About geofignet

What? This web app tries to guess the type of geological figure represented by an image you give it.

Whence? The training extended ResNet-18 using the PyTorch deep-learning framework. It was trained on 1200 images (plus augmentation) with single labels in 20 classes. The validation accuracy was 0.88.

How? There are two ways to use the app: go to the home page and use the form; or use the API.

Why? The app is just for fun. No data whatsoever is stored or transmitted.

What are the classes?

  • blockdiagram — Block diagrams
  • chronostrat — Chronostratigraphic columns
  • corephoto — Core photos
  • correlation — Correlation panels
  • equation — Mathematical equations
  • fieldnote — Field notes and sketches
  • fmilog — Resistivity or conductivity image logs
  • geologicmap — Geological maps
  • outcrop — Outcrop photos
  • photomicrograph — Photomicrographs
  • regionalsection — Regional cross-sections
  • rosediagram — Rose diagrams
  • seismic — Seismic data
  • semicrograph — SEM micrographs
  • stereonet — Stereonets
  • structuremap — Structure maps
  • synthetic — Synthetic seismograms
  • table — Tables
  • ternary — Ternary plots
  • welllog — Well logs
